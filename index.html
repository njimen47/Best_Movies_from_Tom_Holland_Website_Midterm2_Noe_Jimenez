Spider-Man: No Way Home

Summary

One of the finest films in the Marvel cinematic universe is Spider-Man: No Way Home. Tom Holland plays Spiderman, the friendly neighbor whose identity is revealed in this film ("spoiler alert"). Tom Holland performed admirably in the role, and the film received widespread acclaim due to the narrative and the appearance of performers from previous Spiderman films in the same world as Tom Holland's Spiderman.

Onward

Summary

Onward is a Disney Pixar film about two blue trolls who decide to embark on an expedition. Tom Holland did an excellent job depicting everything that occurred to Ian with his voice throughout the film. He put a lot of work into the persona and did an excellent job portraying it. The film contains a significant message that you should appreciate what you have before it is gone, rather than merely lamenting what you've lost.

Uncharted

Summary

Uncharted is a film about a guy on a journey to find billions of dollars in buried riches in far-flung corners of the globe. Nathan Drake, played by Tom Holland, is a wise treasure hunter who has spent practically his entire life alone, searching for the greatest treasure for which his brother left clues. This is an action film, as well as one of the funniest, in which love, thieves, and lies play major roles. Tom Holland clearly understands how to play a variety of roles in all of the films he has been in.
